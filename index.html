05 · Normalization
Verified up to 3NF
1NF
First Normal Form — Atomic Values
Two violations found: languages in FLIGHT_ATTENDANT and nationality in PASSENGER could hold multiple values.
Violation: multi-valued attributesFixed: FLIGHT_ATTENDANT_LANGUAGE & PASSENGER_NATIONALITY tables
2NF
Second Normal Form — No Partial Dependencies
All non-key attributes depend on the full composite key in every table. No violations found.
✓ Already satisfied
3NF
Third Normal Form — No Transitive Dependencies
Three issues resolved: payment_status removed from BOOKING (moved to PAYMENT). AIRCRAFT split to isolate model attributes. LOYALTY_ACCOUNT tier logic extracted into LOYALTY_TIER table.
3 transitive dependencies removedFixed: AIRCRAFT_MODEL, LOYALTY_TIER, PAYMENT restructured

06 · Conclusion
What we achieved
🗄️

Clean & Normalized

Our database follows 3NF rules. No duplicate data, no update problems. Everything is stored once in the right place.

🔗

Real Relationships

All business rules are enforced: flights need crew, bookings have payments, passengers collect loyalty points.

🚀

Ready to Use

This design can be implemented in any relational database (MySQL, PostgreSQL, etc.). It is scalable and easy to maintain.
